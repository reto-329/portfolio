About

Professor Oluwasola S. Maitamni is an Associate Professor of Computer Science (Software Engineering) from Babcock University with a track record of 20 years in Higher Education in Nigeria. He is the current HOD of Software Engineering Department, Babcock University, Ilishan Remo, Ogun State, Nigeria, and Deputy Director Academics, Babcock University Center for Open and Distance e-learning (BUCODeL).

He earned his MSc and PhD from Babcock University and his Post-Doctoral Research in Andrews University, Berrien Springs, Michigan, USA. He is a proficient writer with multiple publications and research interests in Cyber Physical Systems, Distance Learning Education, Learning Management Systems, and Human Computer Interaction.
